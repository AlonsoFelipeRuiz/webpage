Who am I?

I am a dedicated researcher with extensive experience in molecular biology, bioinformatics, and structural biology. Over the past years, I have contributed to various research projects and gained valuable insights from my academic and professional journey.

My journey begins with my bachelor studies in Biochemistry and Biomedical Sciences, followed by a master's degree in Molecular Approaches in Health Sciences and a doctoral thesis in the prestigious group of Alberto Marina at the Institute of Biomedicine of Valencia. From October 2019 to November 2023 as a predoctoral researcher at the Institute of Biomedicine of Valencia, I acquired a deep knowledge of "Quorum Sensing" systems from functional and structural perspectives, covering structural biology, bioinformatics, as well as fundamental aspects of microbiology.

At the Institute of Biomedicine of Valencia, I developed expertise in Python coding for bioinformatic applications, enabling me to analyze complex biological data effectively, with a strong background in structural biology, structure analysis and prediction focusing in the field of microbiology and virology.

In September 2020, during my PhD I joined the Microbial Evolutionary Genomics Unit at the Institute Pasteur for a two month internship, where I delved into comparative genomics and microbiology. Here, I further honed my skills in the generation of Hidden Markov Models (HMM) and gained insights into microbial evolution and phylogenetics.

A second PhD stay at Imperial College from July 2022 to September 2022, immersed myself in the fascinating world of genetic engineering and virology. My experience in bacterial modification and molecular biology techniques broadened my understanding of microbial systems and their applications in various fields.

Driven by a passion for learning and innovation, I pursued further education, obtaining certifications in Python programming and cloud computing. My academic journey culminated in pursuing a Ph.D. in Biomedicine and Biotechnology.

Since Februrary 2024 I work as chief technology officer (CTO) at Evolving Therapeutics, were I use my strong foundation in biochemistry and biomedical sciences, coupled with advanced skills in bioinformatics and data analysis, to leveraging technology and scientific innovation to address key challenges in healthcare and biotechnology for phage therapy.
